Choose the Starting Hand Strength

You need yo decide if your poker hand is considered Playable or Garbage.

All players are sitting with 100 Big Blinds chip stacks.

Playable are hands you can play given the right circumstances.

Garbage are hands you would never consider playing.

Open-Raise From Under The Gun

In this scenario, you are first to act, and need to decide what to do with your hand.

You are in an Early Position and dacing 5 more active players, so you should play a Very Tight Range of cards.

When opening the Pot, you should always Raise or Fold. Electing to jsut Call is usually a losing strategy.

Without information on your Opponents, you assume taht they are Passive and play Too Many hands.

Under The Gun Facing Re-Raise (Without Position)

In this scenario, you Open-Raised the Pot and an Opponent who is In Position to you, Re-Raised you.

When you Open-Raised, you represented a Very Tight Range, so now you should Defend with a Super Tight Range.

When Defending vs. Re-Raise while Out of Position, it is better to Re-Raise or Fold.

Calling will put you in a tough spot, because you Lock Initiative and first to act.

Without information on your Opponents, you assume that they are Passive and play Too Many hands.

Use the chart below to know which hands you should Re-Raise, and if you get Re-Raised again, which hands you should go All-In with.
